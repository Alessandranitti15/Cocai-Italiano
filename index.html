Our video-courses

The courses are designed for complete beginners or for those who already have some basic knowledge of the Italian language.

At the end of the courses, you will be able to order food, take the bus or the train, ask for information, talk about yourself and what you like, and meet new friends! Italy will finally have no secrets for you.

Are you an absolute beginner but long to be able to speak some Italian? Then the "Italian for beginners" video course is the best choice you can make to start learning this beautiful language.

This course is designed for those who already have some basic knowledge of the Italian language. At the end of the course, you will be able to communicate about yourself and the world around you. And, of course, to order Italian coffee!

Video lessons

Teacher Alessandra will lead you step by step and help you learn daily expressions, build a useful Italian vocabulary and grammar fundamentals.

Practicing

The interactive exercises will make you speak as soon as you learn new words and expressions. Moreover, at the end of every video you are welcome to test yourself.
